Frequently asked questions

Clear answers while we are still early.

Is Naviquo available now?

No. Naviquo is currently in research and concept development. The forum pages are static previews and the public app is not yet available.

Will Naviquo be free?

Our current intention is to keep the core crew experience free. Final plans will depend on development costs, partnerships and user research.

Why are you collecting an email address?

It lets us manage research responses, contact you when necessary and invite volunteers to testing where they have opted in. We do not ask for employee IDs, passport details or sensitive documents.

Can I ask for my response to be deleted?

Yes. The Privacy Policy explains how to contact Naviquo and request access, correction or deletion.

Are the cruise-line guides official?

The planned guides would contain user-submitted experiences and would need clear moderation and disclaimers. They would not represent official statements from cruise lines.
