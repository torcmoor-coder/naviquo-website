Frequently asked questions

Clear about where we are now.

Is the app available?

No. Naviquo is currently in research and early concept development. Public launch dates have not been confirmed.

Will it be free?

The current plan is to keep the main crew experience free, while exploring responsible partner and business revenue later.

Are all listed features guaranteed?

No. Features will be prioritised based on crew demand, technical feasibility, safety, cost and testing.
